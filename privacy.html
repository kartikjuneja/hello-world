Overview

TradeGun is built to keep your data private. This policy explains what information is processed by the extension and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

TradeGun stores only extension settings and preferences in your browser using Chrome local storage APIs. We do not collect personally identifiable information for resale or profiling.

Permissions and Access

  • We request minimal permissions necessary for extension functionality.
  • Storage permission is used only to save your feature preferences locally.
  • Host permissions are limited to Binance pages and TradeGun service endpoints required for operation.

Data Sharing and Sales

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share user data with third parties for advertising purposes.
